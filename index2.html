Head Chef Overview

You are a scrappy startup chef trying to break into the big time. Work your way up from a hot dog stand to owning a chain of restaurants and more. Hire workers, buy ingredients and open stores.

This game is a Fast Clicker. It is designed to last a short while - perhaps a hour or two, and you can play several times to work out the best strategy.

Note: Your customers start turning up immediately. If there are too many in a line, they will get annoyed, leave and might complain to their friends. Start by clicking "serve a hot dog". On the other hand, sometimes looking busy is good too..…
